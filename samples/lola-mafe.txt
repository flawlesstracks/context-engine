Lola Mafe is a creative director and brand strategist based in Atlanta. She married CJ Mitchell on February 14 2023 and they have an unconventional arrangement where they live in separate homes two blocks apart. Lola previously worked at Ogilvy and McCann before going independent in 2021. She specializes in brand identity for tech startups and has worked with over 30 early-stage companies. She holds a BFA from Savannah College of Art and Design. Lola is originally from Lagos Nigeria and moved to the US at age 12. She speaks Yoruba and English fluently. Her close collaborators include Nia Osei a UX designer in Brooklyn and Tariq Adams a copywriter she frequently partners with. Lola values authenticity in branding and believes most startups over-design and under-communicate. She prefers direct honest feedback and is known for telling clients what they need to hear not what they want to hear.
